Hotel Room Block

A block of hotel rooms has been reserved at the Lorem Ipsum Resort. You will provide your hotel accommodation details during the online registration process. Please do not contact the hotel directly.

Arrival Policy

All attendees are expected to arrive on Sunday, Lorem 17. Lorem Ipsum will pay for the nights of Sunday, Lorem 17; Monday, Lorem 18; Tuesday, Lorem 19; and Wednesday, Lorem 20 (check-out on Thursday, Lorem 21).

Extensions

If you would like to request to extend your stay beyond your approved dates, you may contact the SKO FY27 Registration team at sko@ntnxevents.com. All extensions will be at your own expense, and no exceptions will be made.

Please note: Any pre- and post-extensions are not guaranteed by the hotel and are subject to hotel availability.

Internet Access

In-room wireless internet access is included in the nightly room rate. Instructions on accessing the guest room internet will be provided upon check-in at the hotel. Wi-Fi will be provided in all meeting spaces.

Smoking Policy

Smoking is not permitted in guest rooms or public spaces. Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas. Any fees associated with nonadherence to the smoking policy are the responsibility of the individual.

Gratuity Guidelines

Gratuities should be paid at your own discretion and expensed per the Lorem Ipsum Travel & Expense Policy.

Hotel Charges

A valid credit card is required by the hotel upon check-in for all hotel charges not covered. Upon check-in, you will be charged for any room nights not covered, along with applicable taxes and fees. Additionally, a hold of $150.00 per day, up to a maximum of 4 nights, or $600.00 total, will be placed on your credit card for incidentals.

Cancellation Policy

Any changes or cancellations to your hotel reservation must be sent to sko@ntnxevents.com. Failure to cancel pre- or post-extension reservations at least 72 hours prior to your arrival will result in a penalty fee of one night's room charge plus taxes and fees.

Hotel Accessibility

The Lorem Ipsum Resort provides accessible rooms. If you require an accessible room, please contact sko@ntnxevents.com.
